Chocoholics Anonymous System Design

High cohesion and low coupling are both included in our design, making it very efficient. It makes use of sequential and verbal coherence. The three different menus (for each user: provider, manager, and operator) that follow the main menu exhibit sequential cohesion. In addition, our design also incorporates temporal cohesion because we have the timer functions as an actor in this system. Since every use case has its own classes and functions, our design as a whole also features a high level of functional cohesion. Though undesirable, some coupling is included in our design. We have external coupling, which was unavoidable since all functions must be used in the same terminal. Also, it uses some stamp and data coupling when accessing records and printing reports; these are the loosest types of coupling and will increase productivity. Overall, the design is solid and effective.
